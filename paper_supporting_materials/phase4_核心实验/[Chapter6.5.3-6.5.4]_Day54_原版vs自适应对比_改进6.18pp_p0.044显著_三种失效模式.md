### 代码层面分析

**原版Strategy #13的入场条件**:
```python
# 条件1: SMA5上穿SMA10
sma5_cross_sma10 = (self.sma_5[0] > self.sma_10[0] and
                    self.sma_5[-1] <= self.sma_10[-1])

# 条件2: RSI > 35
rsi_filter = self.rsi[0] > self.params.rsi_threshold  # 35

# 条件3: SMA20 > SMA10 (趋势过滤)
trend_filter = self.sma_20[0] > self.sma_10[0]

# 全部满足才入场

    self.buy(size=20)  # 固定20股
```
